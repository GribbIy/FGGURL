Our Views on Social VR

Introducing Our Version of Social VR

We are dedicated to delivering an immersive virtual reality experience that is both inclusive and engaging. Our platform is designed to mirror the dynamism and diversity of the real world, allowing users to interact and express themselves in a vibrant digital environment.

We adhere to a set of guidelines similar to those found on leading social media platforms, such as X (formerly Twitter), ensuring a respectful and enjoyable experience for all users. Our platform is tailored for individuals aged 13 and older, with specialized features for mature audiences aged 18 and above. It provides a space where users can authentically express their thoughts and opinions while enjoying the freedom to be themselves.

Users can create and share their own virtual content, from custom environments to interactive experiences, offering opportunities to showcase creativity and engage with others. Additionally, the platform facilitates meaningful social connections, enabling users to meet and interact with new people in the virtual space.

Please note that our platform is primarily optimized for Oculus/Meta devices. While we extend compatibility to other systems such as Steam VR, users may encounter occasional technical issues. For the best experience, we recommend using Oculus/Meta devices. When you email please send a discord username or a social media we are able to contact you with!!
