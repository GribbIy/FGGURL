Our Values

Inclusivity for All

At GDS, we are committed to creating an environment where everyone feels welcomed and respected, regardless of their background, identity, or experiences.

Support for LGBTQ+ Rights

We stand with the LGBTQ+ community and advocate for equal rights and representation, ensuring that all voices are heard and celebrated.

Empowering Women and Men

We believe in gender equality and strive to support women and men in all aspects of life, fostering a space where their contributions are valued.

Racial Justice

GDS supports movements like Black Lives Matter and stands against racial injustice, promoting diversity and equality in everything we do.

Celebrating Cultural Diversity

We embrace the richness that Asian, Indigenous, Latinx, and other cultural identities bring to our world, encouraging a multicultural perspective in our community.
